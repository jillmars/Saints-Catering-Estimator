Saints Pizza Pub

Back in 1995 a bunch of guys were tired of the lack of a fun pub atmosphere where they could watch a game with their buddies, bring their kids for lunch, or even take their wives for a quality beer and a great meal... So they decided to do it themselves and Saints Pizza Pub was born. Featuring a great selection of hand-crafted menu items, a range of seasonal specials, craft beers on tap and a friendly staff able to concoct a high-end cocktail, Saints soon became a neighborhood favorite. Come see us at 131 Breckenridge Avenue in the heart of St. Matthews and find out for yourself why so many have been coming to see us for so long.
